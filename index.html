Meg Roebling

Bio

I'm a multi-disciplinary digital designer specializing in user centered design and research. I advocate for design thinking and believe in testing often and early. I design for desktop, tablet, mobile and large touchscreen. Clients include AIG, American Express, Verizon, Details, Discover and Christie's.

I created and ran, for three years, Reach Adoption, a company providing online learning, parent profiles and outreach campaigns for those seeking to adopt. The company provided may successful matches between hopeful parents and pregnant women before closing in 2016.

My digital design and development consulting company open since 2000 is Elysium Media. An offshoot enterprise is Patagus Enterprises, providing NYC tour mobile apps.
